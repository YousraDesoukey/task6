About Us

OUR VISION
Our main vision to keep up with the immensely accelerated
innovation in automotive technology worldwide by: researching,
learning and applying this knowledge to revive the automotive
industry in Egypt.
OUR MISSION
- To establish an advanced Egyptian research entity with divisions concerned with Automotive Engineering and Mobile Robotics.
- With 90% of our car MADE IN EGYPT, designed and manufactured by undergraduate engineering students at the ASU Racing Team, we are trying to deliver a message: Egyptian youth has the capabilities and creativity required to lead and innovate! All we need is a little push. (And we may add a foot pressing the gas paddle after this last line. However, you may find it cheesy.)
